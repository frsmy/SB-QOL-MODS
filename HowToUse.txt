*You must have installed and ran Forge 1.8.9*
Install Pizza_Client-1.1.3.jar
Do Windows+R to open the "Run" menu
Type in %appdata%
Go to "Roaming" an then go to ".minecraft"
Open the "mods" folder and drag Pizza_Client-1.1.3.jar in it
Launch Forge 1.8.9, connect to Hypixel
To open the gui do /pizza
Enjoy !
